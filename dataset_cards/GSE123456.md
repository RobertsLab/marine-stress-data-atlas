## Experimental design

- Control group: ambient seawater (~15 C), n = 12
- Treatment group: heat stress (30 C), n = 12
- Gill tissue sampled after 5 days of continuous exposure
- Two-factor design (temperature × time) with 3 biological replicates per cell

## What the data contain

- 24 RNA-seq libraries
- Raw FASTQ files plus a gene-level count matrix
- Reads aligned to the *C. gigas* reference assembly
